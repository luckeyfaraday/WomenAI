# WomenAI

## Concept
An AI assistant specifically designed to cater to the needs, preferences, and challenges faced by women.

## Status
- **Phase**: Ideation
- **Date Created**: 2026-01-18

## 1. Core Value Proposition
A general-purpose AI assistant optimized to provide emotional support and address woman-specific problems, alongside standard AI capabilities.

## 2. Target Audience
Any woman who uses AI assistants.

## 3. Key Domains (Initial Focus)
The initial development will focus on two critical domains:
- **Health**: Providing information, tracking, and support for women's health issues.
- **Safety**: Offering features and resources to enhance personal safety.

## 4. Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Features

Based on balancing impact and feasibility, the initial MVP will include:

### Health Domain
-   **Menstrual Cycle Tracking**: Features for tracking periods, predicting cycles, and providing relevant insights.
-   **Mental Wellness Support**: Tools for mood tracking, guided meditations, and resources for mental well-being.

### Safety Domain
-   **Safety Resources Info**: A knowledge base providing information on local safety resources, self-defense tips, and emergency contacts.
